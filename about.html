About CloseTheOffer

The job search dashboard that takes you from application to offer.

Job hunting is a project, but most people manage it with a messy spreadsheet, a cluttered inbox, and a lot of mental overhead. Applications fall through the cracks, follow-ups get forgotten, and it's hard to see the whole pipeline at a glance.

CloseTheOffer brings it all into one place: pipeline tracking, interview prep, Gmail sync, and job-board search — so you always know what to do next.

What we believe

A job search should feel organized, not overwhelming. You should own your data. And the tools that help you should be fast, private, and genuinely useful — not another thing to maintain.

Who we are

CloseTheOffer is built by a small team that has been through the job-search grind and wanted something better. We ship continuously and read every piece of feedback.

Have an idea or a question? We'd love to hear from you at hello@closetheoffer.com.
